About

Petros Pechlivanoglou is a Senior Scientist at the Hospital for Sick Children (SickKids) and an Associate Professor at the Institute of Health Policy, Management and Evaluation (IHPME), University of Toronto. His research focuses on methods and applications of decision analysis in health economics, integrating evidence synthesis, administrative data, survival and multistate modeling, and value-of-information analysis.

  • MSc, Econometrics — University of Groningen
