Projects & Themes

Decision & Economic Modelling Methods

  • Developing open-source R packages and workflows for model calibration, extrapolation, and value-of-information analysis.
  • Comparisons between partitioned survival vs. multistate modelling frameworks under heterogeneity and limited data.
  • Methodological work on semi-Markov models, time‑dependent covariates, and sojourn‑time effects.

Applications in Child & Oncology Economics

  • Long-term cost‑effectiveness and survivorship modelling in paediatric oncology, including late effects, QoL, and mortality transitions.
  • Economics of preterm birth prevention and long-term impact on families, health costs, and outcomes.
  • Policy analyses using administrative data to estimate the economic burden of childhood conditions and interventions.

Evidence Synthesis & Policy‑Driven Analysis

  • Meta-analysis, network meta-analysis, and population-adjusted indirect comparisons in HTA settings.
  • Bridging administrative data, clinical trial results, and decision models to inform reimbursement decisions.
  • Engaging in priority-setting, research value-of-information, and methodological guidance for health agencies.
